The First Offer Blueprint walks you through defining exactly what you'd sell, who you'd sell it to, and what you'd charge — in one focused session. It's free. It's structured. And it does the thing that most people never do: gets your offer on paper.

What It Is

The First Offer Blueprint is a free, step-by-step build guide — not a PDF full of advice, not a worksheet with prompts, and not a mini-course with a ten-day drip sequence.

It's a structured process. You work through it once. You come out the other side with a defined offer. Specifically: one clear offer with a name, a buyer, a price, and a one-sentence pitch. That's the output. That's the goal. That's what you'll have when you're done.

It takes most people under two hours.

What's Inside

Six Parts. One Output.

1
What to Sell
Three prompts that surface your best offer idea from what you already know, have already done, or are already being asked about. This isn't brainstorming — it's excavation. The offer is usually already there.
2
Who It's For
A one-paragraph buyer definition exercise. You'll write down who this person is, what they're struggling with, and why they'd pay to solve it. If you can't do this, your offer isn't ready. This section makes sure you can.
3
What to Charge
A straightforward pricing framework — not a formula, a process. It walks through what the right price signals about your offer and why starting too low is as risky as starting too high.
4
What the Offer Includes
A deliverable list template. You define exactly what someone gets when they buy — the specific outputs, the format, the timeline. Vague offers don't sell. This section makes yours concrete.
5
Your Offer in One Sentence
A fill-in-the-blank structure that produces your core positioning sentence. This is what you say when someone asks what you do. It's also the foundation of every piece of marketing you'll write later.
6
Your Next Step
A clear, specific recommendation for what to do next based on where your offer lands — build it yourself, use Launch Ready, or get direct feedback in The Launch Cohort (coming soon).
Who This Is For

This is for you if:

  • You've had an idea for a business, service, or product for more than six months and haven't launched it
  • You know what you're good at but you've never turned it into something someone can buy
  • You've started something before and stalled before making a single sale
  • You want to start earning from your skills or expertise but don't know what your offer even is
  • You've bought tools, courses, or resources and still don't have anything in the market

This is NOT for you if:

  • You already have a working offer and paying clients
  • You're looking for a guaranteed fast path to passive income
