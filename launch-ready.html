The Problem

Why capable people don't launch.

They have an idea. They build some things — a logo, a website, a rough outline. They hit the first wall — usually the sales page or the pitch — and they stall. They don't know how to write about their offer without sounding like everyone else. They don't know what to charge. They don't know who to reach out to first or what to say.

So they go back to learning. More YouTube. More blogs. Another resource. And still nothing ships.

The root problem isn't knowledge. It's that no one showed them the specific sequence of things that need to exist before an offer can sell. Sales page. Positioning. Pricing rationale. First outreach list. Initial pitch. Launch window. Those things have to be built in a specific order — and most people try to do them all at once and wonder why it doesn't come together.

Launch Ready is that sequence. Built out, step by step - with specific frameworks and prompts at each stage. You use your own ChatGPT or Claude to do the drafting. We give you exactly what to ask and in what order.

ChatGPT (and Claude) will answer whatever you ask. If you know exactly what to ask - in what order, with what context, structured toward what output - they're powerful tools. Most people don't know what to ask. Or they ask the right questions in the wrong order. Or they get a good-looking answer that doesn't actually fit their offer or their buyer.

Launch Ready is the structure. It's a sequenced system with specific steps, specific outputs, and specific prompts built for each stage. You're still using your own ChatGPT or Claude.
